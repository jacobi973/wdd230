Overview of Boise Chamber Of Commerce

We are the Boise Metro Chamber, and we serve as the region's advocate for a vibrant and prosperous economy and outstanding quality of life. The programs and services we provide supports four core areas: economic vitality, advocacy, member education, and member engagement. We currently serve 1,850 member businesses representing 120,000+ employees through the Boise Metro area.
