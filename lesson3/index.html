Site Purpose

Storm Funnel provides the latest information on any tornado warnings or hurricane that could strike your area. It also provides up to date weather in whatever region of the United States you are located in. Sign up for email or text alerts for the latest information and to keep you posted on any potential weather danger in your area.

With a simple layout and easy navigation, the people are able to gleam information right away.
